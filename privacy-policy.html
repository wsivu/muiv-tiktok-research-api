Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 10.02.2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the MGSU TikTok Research Tool ("we", "our", "the Tool") handles data in compliance with academic research standards and platform requirements.

2. Data We Access

Through TikTok Research API, we access only:

  • Publicly available TikTok content
  • Aggregated, anonymized statistics
  • Non-personal engagement metrics (likes, shares, views)
  • Public hashtag and trend data

We do NOT access:

  • Private user information
  • Direct messages
  • Phone numbers or email addresses
  • Location data beyond country-level (if public)

3. How We Use Data

All data is used exclusively for:

  • Statistical analysis for academic research
  • Identifying content trends and patterns
  • Creating aggregated reports
  • Academic publications and presentations

4. Data Storage and Retention

Our data handling practices:

  • Storage: Data is stored on secure university servers
  • Retention: Research data is retained for 2 years as required by academic standards
  • Deletion: All raw data is deleted after research completion
  • Anonymization: All outputs are fully anonymized

5. Data Protection

We implement:

  • University-grade security protocols
  • Access controls (only research team members)
  • Regular security assessments
  • Encryption for stored data

6. Third-Party Compliance

We adhere to:

  • TikTok Developer Terms
  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • GDPR (for EU data subjects)
  • Russia's data protection laws

7. User Rights

Since we only process public data, individual requests should be directed to:

  • TikTok's data request portal for platform data
  • Our contact for research methodology questions

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in research methodology or compliance requirements. Significant changes will be documented.

This privacy policy is valid only for the academic research use of TikTok Research API.
