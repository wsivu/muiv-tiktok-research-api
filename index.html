About This Project

This is an official academic research initiative conducted by MUIV. Our project focuses on analyzing social media trends and patterns using TikTok's Research API for educational purposes.

Research Goals

Understanding content virality, engagement patterns, and platform dynamics in social media ecosystems.

Methodology

Statistical analysis of aggregated, anonymized data obtained through official TikTok APIs.

Data Usage & Privacy

Our research strictly adheres to ethical guidelines and platform policies:

  • Public Data Only: We analyze only publicly available content
  • Anonymization: All data is aggregated and anonymized
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect or store personal user data
  • Limited Retention: Research data is retained only for academic purposes
  • Non-Commercial: This research is not for commercial use
